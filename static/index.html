How to interpret this comparison

Lexicon vs Deep Learning

NLTK VADER calculates sentiment by looking up words in a hand-curated vocabulary (lexicon) and adding their intensity ratings. It works well with exclamation marks, capitalization, and emoji ratings but lacks deep context understanding.

Contextual Awareness

RoBERTa uses multi-head self-attention mechanisms to analyze relationships between words. This lets it detect sarcasm, double-negatives, and overall document semantic tone that simple dictionary lookups miss.
